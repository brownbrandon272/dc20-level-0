# Maneuvers and Spellcasting

Martials

Stamina Points

**Your 1 Stamina Point can be spent in place of 1 Action Point for Maneuvers.  You regain your Stamina Point either at the end of combat or by spending 2 Action Points.**

Maneuvers

**You get all Attack Maneuvers and 3 additional maneuvers of your choice.**

_Attack Maneuvers_

- _Extend Attack -_ **Maneuver: (1 AP)** Your Melee Attack Range is increased by 1 Space (or your Ranged Attack Range is increased by 5 Spaces) for the Attack check.
- _Power Attack -_ **Maneuver: (1 AP)** You deal +1 damage with the Attack. You can use this Maneuver multiple times.
- _Sweep Attack_ - **Maneuver: (1 AP)** Choose 1 additional target within 1 Space of the original target that's within your Attack Range. Make 1 Attack Check against all targets. **Attack Hit:** The original target takes your Attack's damage, and each additional target Hit takes 1 damage of the same type.

_Save Maneuvers_

- _Expose -_ **Maneuver: (1 AP)** The target becomes **Exposed** (Attacks against it have ADV) against the next Attack made against it before the end of your next turn.
- _Hamstring -_ **Maneuver: (1 AP)** The target is **Slowed** (every 1 Space you move costs an extra 1 Space of movement) until the end of your next turn.
- _Trip_ - **Maneuver: (2 AP)** The target falls **Prone**.

_Defense Maneuvers_

- _Parry -_ **Trigger:** When a creature you can see within 1 Space (including yourself) is targeted by an Attack against its PD. **Reaction (Maneuver): (1 AP)** You grant the target a +5 bonus to PD against this Attack.
- _Raise Shield -_ **Requires:** You're wielding a shield. **Trigger:** A creature within 1 Space (including yourself) is targeted by an Attack against its PD. **Reaction (Maneuver): (1 AP)** Reduce the damage against the target by an amount equal to your Shield's PD bonus.
- _Taunt -_ **Maneuver: (1 AP)** Choose an enemy creature that can see or hear you within 5 Spaces. Make an Attack Check, Influence Check, or Intimidation Check (your choice) contested by the target's Mental Save. **Contest Success:** The target is **Taunted** (DisADV on Attacks against creatures other than you) by you on their next Attack. **Full Taunt:** You can spend an additional 1 AP to have the **Taunted** Condition last until the end of your next turn

_Grapple Maneuvers_

- _Body Block -_ **Trigger:** You are targeted by an Attack. **Reaction (Maneuver):** (1 AP)
- You reposition a creature Grappled by you to shield yourself from damage.
- **Effect:** You and the Grappled creature each take half the damage dealt by the attack.
- **Aftermath:** You can move the Grappled creature to any adjacent space immediately.
- _Restrain -_ **Maneuver:** (1 AP)
- The target is **Restrained** until the Grapple ends.
- **Target Options:** On its turn, the target can spend 1 AP to end the Restrained condition but remains Grappled.
- _Throw -_ **Requires:** The Grappled creature is your size or smaller. **Maneuver:** (1 AP)
- The target is thrown up to a number of spaces equal to **Â½ of your Might**, ending the Grappled condition.
- **Additional Range:** Add 1 extra space for every **5 points** the target fails the Contest Check by.

Casters

**Choose a spell list:**

Lightning & Teleportation List

_Lightning Bolt_

**Destruction (Cantrip)  
Cost:** 1 AP  
**Range:** 10 Spaces  
**Duration:** Instantaneous

**Effects**

- **Spell Attack:** Make a Spell Check against the PD of a target within range.  
    **Hit:** The target takes **2 Lightning damage**.
- **Lightning Orb:** Create crackling lightning between your hands for up to **10 minutes**, shedding Bright Light in a **10 Space radius**. The energy is harmless to you and your equipment.  
    The Spell ends early if you:
  - Dismiss it (no cost).
  - Cast it again.
  - Stop using both hands.
  - Spend 1 AP to make a Spell Attack with it.
- **Cantrip Passive:** Deal +1 damage to creatures wearing metal armor.

**Action Point Enhancements**

- **Damage (1 AP):** +1 Lightning damage.
- **Range (1 AP):** Increase range by **+5 Spaces**.
- **Chain (1 AP):** Target 1 additional creature within **2 Spaces** of the previous target.
  - Make a separate Spell Check against the new target.
  - **Hit:** The additional target takes **2 Lightning damage** (not affected by extra damage sources like Heavy Hits or higher-level effects).
  - This Enhancement can be used multiple times, chaining to additional targets within 2 Spaces of the previous one.

_Crackling Lightning_

**Destruction  
Cost:** 2 AP + 1 MP  
**Range:** Self (10 Spaces)  
**Duration:** Instantaneous

Crackling lightning forms around you. Choose a type of area: **Line, Cone, or Sphere.** You are the Spell's Point of Origin.

- **Line:** Affects every target in a **1 Space wide and 10 Space long** area.
- **Cone:** Affects every target in a **3 Space long Cone**.
- **Sphere:** Affects every target within a **2 Space radius**.

Make a Spell Check against each target's PD within the area.

- **Hit:** Targets take **2 Lightning damage**.

**Mana Point Enhancements**

- **Frazzled (1 MP):** Targets must Save against the **Dazed Condition** for 1 minute.
- **Range (1 MP):** Change the Spell's origin point to **15 Spaces** (instead of Self).

_Misty Step_

**Astromancy  
Cost:** 1 AP + 1 MP  
**Range:** Self  
**Duration:** Instantaneous

Briefly surrounded by silvery mist, you attempt to teleport to a new location.

Make a DC 20 Spell Check.

- **Success:** Teleport up to **5 Spaces** to an unoccupied space you can see.
  - For every 5 points above the DC, increase the teleport distance by **+2 Spaces**.
- **Failure:** Teleport **3 Spaces** instead.

**Mana Point Enhancements**

- **Far Step (1 MP):** Increase the teleport distance by **+4 Spaces**.

Psychic & Enchantment List

_Psi Bolt_

**Destruction (Cantrip)  
Cost:** 1 AP  
**Range:** 10 Spaces  
**Duration:** Instantaneous

**Effects**

- **Spell Attack:** Make a Spell Check against the MD of a target within range.  
    **Hit:** The target takes **1 Psychic damage**.
- **Headache:** Cause a mild headache in one creature you can see within range for **1 minute**, or spend 1 AP to make a Spell Attack.

**Cantrip Passive**

- Deal +1 damage to creatures that are **Dazed**.

**Action Point Enhancements**

- **Damage (1 AP):** +1 Psychic damage.
- **Range (1 AP):** Increase range by **+5 Spaces**.
- **Dazed (1 AP):** Target makes a Mental Save.  
    **Failure:** The target becomes **Dazed** (DisADV on Mental Checks) on its next Mental Check before the end of your next turn.

_Psychic Fear_

**Enchantment  
Cost:** 2 AP + 1 MP  
**Range:** 10 Spaces  
**Duration:** Instantaneous

Whisper a discordant melody to one creature you can see within range, causing it terrible pain.

- Make a Spell Check against the target's MD while it makes an **Intelligence Save**.  
    **Hit:** The target takes **2 Psychic damage**.  
    **Save Failure:** If the target has any AP, it spends **1 AP** to move as far as its Speed allows away from you. The creature avoids obviously dangerous ground (e.g., fire or pits).

**Mana Point Enhancements**

- **Loud Whispers (1 MP):** The creature loses an additional **1 AP** (if available) and moves that many extra Spaces away.

_Command_

**Enchantment  
Cost:** 2 AP + 1 MP  
**Range:** 10 Spaces  
**Duration:** 1 Round

Speak a command to a creature within range that can hear you.

- The creature doesn't need to be visible to you if you've seen it within the last minute.
- Make a Spell Check contested by the target's **Charisma Save**.  
    **Success:** The target immediately spends **2 AP** to follow your command, regardless of the usual AP cost. The creature also cannot spend any resources (AP, SP, MP) to modify the action.

**Ignoring a Command**

The spell has no effect if the target:

- Doesn't understand your language.
- Is unable to follow the command.
- Would be harmed directly by following the command.

**Choosing a Command**

You can choose from the following example commands or improvise with GM approval:

- **Move:** The target moves up to its Speed to a location or direction of your choice.
- **Prone:** The target falls **Prone**.
- **Drop:** The target drops anything it's holding.
- **Attack:** The target makes **1 Attack Check** or **Spell Check** (your choice) that costs 1 AP. You choose the target, which must be within the creature's range.

**Mana Point Enhancements**

- **Duration (1 MP):** Increase the duration by **1 Round**.
- **Targets (2 MP):** Add **1 additional target**.

Holy & Restoration List

_Sacred Bolt_

**Destruction (Cantrip)  
Cost:** 1 AP  
**Range:** 10 Spaces  
**Duration:** Instantaneous

**Effects**

- **Spell Attack:** Make a Spell Check against the MD of a target within range.  
    **Hit:** The target takes **1 Radiant damage**.
- **Bright Beam:** Cause a subtle light to descend upon a creature you can see within range, making it glow for **10 minutes** or make a Spell Attack.

**Cantrip Passive**

- Deal +1 Radiant damage to **Undead** and **Exposed** creatures.

**Action Point Enhancements**

- **Damage (1 AP):** +1 Radiant damage.
- **Range (1 AP):** Increase the range by **+5 Spaces**.
- **Holy Bolt (1 AP):** The target makes a Mental Save.  
    **Failure:** The target becomes **Exposed** (Attack Checks against it have ADV) until the end of your next turn or until the next Attack Check made against it.

_Bless_

**Divination  
Cost:** 2 AP + 1 MP  
**Range:** 5 Spaces  
**Duration:** 1 minute (Concentration)

Make a **DC 10 Spell Check**.

- **Success:** Bless up to **3 creatures** of your choice within range.
  - For every **10 points** above the DC, bless **+1 additional creature**.
- **Failure:** Bless only **2 creatures**.

**Effect**

While the spell lasts, whenever a blessed target makes a **Check** or **Save**, it can roll a d4 and add the result to the total.

**Mana Point Enhancements**

- **Targets (1 MP):** Increase the number of targets by **+1**.
- **Grace (2 MP):** Change the die rolled from a **d4** to a **d6**.

_Heal_

**Restoration  
Cost:** 2 AP + 1 MP  
**Range:** 10 Spaces  
**Duration:** Instantaneous

Make a **DC 10 Spell Check**.

- **Success:** Heal a creature within range for **3 HP**.
  - For every **5 points** above the DC, heal an additional **+1 HP**.
  - On a **Nat 20**, heal an additional **+2 HP**.
- **Failure:** Heal for only **2 HP**.

**Additional Effect**

If touching the target when you cast this spell, it heals for an additional **1 HP**.

**Mana Point Enhancements**

- **Quick Heal (1 MP):** Reduce the AP cost by **1 AP**.

**Bolster:** Spend **1 MP** to increase healing by **+3 HP** for each MP spent.
